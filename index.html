The Wen Foundation

The Wen Foundation, led by the Ovols NFT community, is a group dedicated to giving back to the Solana community by supporting the development of public goods and further amplifying the message of what is only possible on Solana.

The Wen Foundation’s first contribution is Wen New Standard (WNS), an open source NFT standard meant to be expanded by any and all members of the community.

There is no team allocation or compensation for early contributors. With a simple breakdown of 70% community airdrop and 30% LP/strategic reserve, our core focus is on giving back to Solana
